How to Keep My Google AdSense Account Safe

Many Bloggers who use Google AdSense program on their blog and websites but many don’t understand what it actually means to have a personal Google Adsense account and most commonly this happen because of not reading the terms and webmaster guidelines stated by Adsense Publisher, whether they are using adsense for blogger, wordpress or other they don’t obey the rules properly and lose their approved Adsense account. As far as Google is concerned, there is a business relationship between Google and the publisher, that requires publisher to read and understand Terms and conditions of Adsense and webmaster. Whether you have done that properly or not, here are some very important tips about Adsense for you which you must required to keep in mind from the time you got approved by Google for Adsense Account. (read : when can I apply for Adsense account)   1. Never ever never ever never click on your own ads ! or even don’t encourage anyone to click, Do not tell family and friends that your get money for clicks on Ads on your blog and don’t write or post  anything on your site or blog that can be seen as an inducement to visitors directly or indirectly.   2. Always keep it very very clear in your mind, that if someone using your IP address or connected with it anyway clicks on your ads, and Google disables your Adsense account for this reason, you will never get your account again !   3. As a blogger, keeping all detailed info about your blog as well as Adsense and other important accounts is ultra important, so it is strongly recommended to always keep your eyes on AdSense account and it’s records like number of clicks, page CTR, RPM, CPC etc, also regularly use Google Analytics or other tools you use to monitor your account activity.   4. If ever you detect any kind of unusual account activity like unusual high number of clicks, report about that to Google immediately without wasting even a second, google have provided a special online form for this purpose, in addition also required you to block the domain(s) from which those clicks have been done or disable ads on your site temporarily. If you are not aware and you haven’t taken any steps before termination of your account, chances of getting your Adsense account back would be almost impossible.   5. It’s a matter to give great attention that, If someone clicks on your ads un-necessarily to harm your Adsense account (which generally called as “click bombing”), and Google disables your AdSense account as result, you will be given a chance to file a reconsideration appeal to restore your Adsense account but chances of a successful appeal strictly depends on your ability to prove that you have taken reasonable steps to protect your account.   6. Many know and few don’t, that if you have a AdSense account then you can use ads from your single Adsense account on many website or blog, you don’t need to apply again, means if a person have two or more blogs then he don’t need to apply for Adsense Ads for all his blogs seprately he can use ads from his one Adsense account on multiple blogs and websites he own. However, each blog must comply with AdSense Terms and conditions, remember a person is allowed to have only one AdSense account even two adsense accounts is not allowed to one address.   7. If you have blog and you have Adsense Ads too and there is no Pricay policy on your blog then you can lose your Adsense Account anytime, because all blogs & sites that run AdSense ads are must required to have a privacy policy viewable by all visitors, and your blog must have original content, and also fix it in your mind that posts containing adult content, tobacco and alcohol related contents are strongly prohibited, so promoting such content on your blog can cause serious problems mostly people had paid for this by loosing their Adsense for forever.   8. Some silly people thinks that once they got Adsense it will remian with then forever so generally after receiving Ads they start publishing contents which are not allowed, please tell them their account is safe even if they received payment from Adsense. As every wise man knows, AdSense ads runs on millions of blogs and site, and Google receive enough requests for Ads every second so they prefer to give those ads to someone who use it better instead of giving it to those who cross their lines. So once they feel someone is not obeying their Guidelines they are free to disable Adsense anytime, although warnings are often issued with a short run to get in line before the account is terminated.   9. Last but not least point is that, you must be very careful about the ways of promoting and advertising your website or blog, I mean google also track how you promote your websites and get traffic there, and getting traffic and clicks from Traffic exchanges, buying traffic, arbitration, and even certain forms of use of social networking sites could jeopard your AdSense account. (read : Why google is girl)   Now let me bother you a lil bit more, don’t worry it’s for your goodness, see if keeping Adsense account safe is beyond your ability, then don’t take this source as a stable source of income. In terms of working with AdSense Program seriously, it’s the primary necessity for you to behave like a professional, and even after that all, you’re at the mercy of Google. Self-help and Google’s support forum are the best sources of info. about it’s different aspects. Again I will suggest you to not count income through Adsense Ads as a serious source of income without a dedicated efforts to keep your Adsense account safe & secure. If anything goes wrong and your account is terminated, your only remedy will be an appeal, and if the appeal is rejected, you fu_….d ! The game is over. Finish !!
