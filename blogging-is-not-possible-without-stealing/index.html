Blogging Is Not Possible Without Stealing

Yeah this is true, if you want to be a good blogger and really wants to see your blog on top then you must have to be a good thief, wait don’t take me wrong. I said you need to be a thief but what to steal and how to steal this matters a lot. Many people doing stealing for their blogs and they are doing so well their income through Adsense is huge. Now you may be thinking that I am Crazy, right ? but no I’m neither kidding nor it’s a joke, I’m really saying you to steal, with all presence of my mind. Wow, you still reading this post, good. The real story begins now you must need to steal but not to steal post from any blog, wondered ! it’s OK, it’s Usual, Believe me it’s the key of success in blogging field. Now the question arise if I’m not talking about stealing posts then what’s the thing I’m talking about. Now let me tell you what exactly I’m trying to say, I’m talking about stealing the way of good/top bloggers, means while using internet it happens many times when we see many awesome things on different blogs, then why don’t we steal their idea and use those things on our blog to make it better . Let me give you an example, few days back I was surfing on Internet and visited to a blog there I see that blog was showing a recommended post at right bottom corner when visitors goes down to any post, I liked it and used the same thing on my blog and Guess what happened ? it increases my page views incredibly, see I steal and it helped me and my blog. Original idea not works always, when I started blogging I had many ideas and I wrote on those, many of my those posts are on top of Google’s first page but I haven’t saw any visitors on those posts, you know why, because ideas of those posts were original and unique but nobody searching for them which simply means my hard work and original idea got failed, for proof see this, this post is on Google’s first page from more than 6 months but I never saw any visitor on this post. After some time when I realized my ideas were not working I start following some blogs and bloggers and started writing on the topics they were writing, I followed the way they blog and sincerely follow their guidance, T started following some of them on Facebook and g+ which also helped me a lot. Now you can see the result, today my blog is under 52K alexa world rank and 4K India rank at the age of just 8 months and I think this is great.   From my experience I can say, Stealing is the key of Success for a blogging. Now see what are the things you can steal for blogging and take your blog to a respective position, and those things are.. 
  • You can steal the way they put their contents sometimes this thing become a milestone for blogs.
  • You can steal their post concluding ideas which is most important for writing posts.
  • You can steal their frequency of posting content.
  • You can steal their words bringing call of action .
  • Moral of the story, You can steal the way they blog.
  I spend a lot time on Internet so I learn a lot. I always reading posts of great bloggers and whenever I find something worth stealing I save it in a particular folder, and then by taking ideas from that post I generate a new one, by following this way you will have a huge collection of stolen ideas and you can write about them anytime. Believe me it works, even idea of this post is also stolen :) I hope you getting what I’m trying to say, now if U have any query or suggestion about this or any post of my blog please do comment, It’s like an honor to communicate with my readers.
