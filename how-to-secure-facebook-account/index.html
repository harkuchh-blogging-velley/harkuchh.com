Tip 1 to secure Facebook Account

You must have heard about https, https is more secured than http. https is a communication protocol for secure communication over a computer network. Hence it is always recommended to have an https protocol while surfing on Internet. Every Facebook account has an option of secure browsing, but when we create account on fb it is disabled by default. So all you need to do is enable it!  

Let me tell you the way How to Enable this security setting

  1. click on the down arrow next to the home button then click on the account settings.
  2. click on security at left hand side.
  3. Now in the security settings, click on the very first option named Secure Browsing.
  4. Click on check button in front of  browse Facebook on a secure connection (https) when possible and click on save changes.

Tip 2 to secure Facebook Account

You may know that Gmail has a 2 Step Verification security setting. I personally using this and found it as the best way to secure my account. Now Facebook also providing same way to secure your accounts. With the help of this setting, when you login to Facebook from a different place other than your regular IP, you will receive a text notification on your cell phone/email and it will be very easy for you to know the unauthorized access to your Facebook account.  

To enable this great security Setting on Facebook, Follow these simple steps

  1. Open Account Setting (same page which you opened before).
  2.  Click on Security at left.
  3. Now click on the second option named Login Notification.
  4. Click on Check button and Fill all the required data (Password and your mobile No.)
