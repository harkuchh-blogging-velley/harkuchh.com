How to Increase Website, Blog Traffic and Ranking

How To Increase my Website Traffic or How To Increase  my Blog Traffic ? these are very familiar Question for almost everyone who knows even a little bit about Blogging. Every person owning a blog or website worried always seeking for  improve their blog or website ranking because for earning a good amount of money through blog a decent traffic is much needed but most of the people don’t know the right and efficient way to get Traffic. So here we are providing important tips and tricks by following them in the best way any blog or website can get good traffic and awesome  Ranking, If you really wants to understand this post we suggest you must close every other  application, and read points Carefully, let’s start..  

Use accurate keywords for your content

Keyword means those words which you think when someone write in search engine then engine show link of  your website or blo. Check those with free Google terms and use the most popular relevant one in your title - a descriptive title to let them know right away what it is if it’s a word with multiple meanings. Be very literal and then use the exact keywords several times in the article where they are needed.

Write Articles

This method is highly effective. Not only does it give you immediate traffic, it also gives you  quality backlinks that improve your traffic in the long run. Write an article on a topic relating to your website, and submit it to popular article directories, such as Squidoo, Hubpages, Ezine Articles etc. For example, say you have a blog about love tips. You can write an article on effective love tips, and then mention your own blog in that article. Then submit that article to popular directories for traffic and backlinks, it works a lot. People will read them and visit your website. The best part is that people who read your articles will regard you as an expert, and they will be much more likely to buy from you when they go to your website.  

Use Best Title &  Description

Make it sure that your post  title, description head,  header html tags and URL( uniform Resource locator) accurately convey the subject of your site, by doing this search engine algorithms use these as keywords to categorize your site and direct relevant traffic which help you to get traffic on your website.  

Optimize your website to be search-engine friendly

Submit your site or blog to all various search engines and directories, such as Google, Yahoo, Ask, Bing and MSN, it’s worth submitting to lesser known web directories. If your website is listed in as many places as possible, it can definately boost your rankings in major search engines and also numbers Of visitors will  become to your blog by following this way.

Build Backlinks

Backlinks are very important for a blog, but only the quality ones. Backlinks refer to in-bound links to your blog from other sites. Backlinks give you instant traffic from other sites. But be careful about them. Trust me, you don’t want cheap backlinks to your blog. They will hurt your rank instead of improving it. Never buy a backlink package such as “1,000 backlinks for $9.99” etc. Try to build genuine backlinks on sites with a decent rank. You can also Submit your website to top social bookmarking sites to create a back link to your website, which will increase you page rank for sure.  

Guest Blogging

Encourage guest posting on your blog. There are many advantages of doing that. First of all, it will increase your post frequency It will also reduce your workload and effort. Secondly, it will increase your traffic slightly, since fans from the guest’s blog will want to read what their writer has written on another blog. Be careful about this though. You have to give out backlinks to the guests’ blogs. Too many out going links without a nofollow attribute and your rank will start to drop..! you can also write for others as well. This will get you a backlink. Write multiple posts on quality blogs, and you will see your backlinks increase and your rankings soar! This is one of the best way of building genuine backlinks.  

Networking ( atleast 30 minutes per day)

Networking is essential, especially when you are just getting started. The 30 minutes that you will dedicate to it every day could be split among :
  • commenting on other blogs in your niche,
  • linking to the posts of bloggers in your niche, and
  • interacting with the bloggers in your niche via email, IM or Twitter.

Must remember that your goal is to build genuine relationships, so don’t approach people just because you think they can help to promote your blog. Approach them because you respect their work and because you think the two of you could grow together.

Share Posts on Facebook, Use Friends and Family

Facebook is something which nobody can ignore so always update your Facebook status to let your friends know every time you update your blog. Also create a Facebook fan page. Your friend’s friends will see when they join the fan page or comment on your status and they may also can join your page for further updates.Don’t be afraid or hesitate to share your site address with friends and family who can further distribute your address to many others. They are often your best source of free advertising and traffic boosting, ask them for your help afterall they are your nears and dears and they feel happy by doing so.  

Some more Reliable Tricks are :

 
  • You can also Start your own online discussion community. It could be an online message board, e-mail discussion list or chat room. When people get involved in your community, they will regularly return to communicate with others.
Look into traffic exchanges. There are many types of traffic exchanges but the idea is the same - you team up with other webmasters, and they send you traffic in exchange for you sending them traffic. Search the web for “traffic exchange” to find those services.
  • Getting a myspace page could also increase your site traffic because hundreds of people will see it everyday and if they find something good in it then anybody love to know more and they will visit your site.
Constantly update content on your web-page, so having a news section is a great deal for search engines especially.
  • Sign up to yahoo answers and leave some helpful comments to questions people are asking for, leaving a link back to your site with more helpful tips on it, people love to follow you.
