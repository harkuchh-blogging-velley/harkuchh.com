Dakhal.in, India’s First Social Network by Pair of Son-Dad

This is quite interesting news for all especially for Indians. A team of a son-father developed India’s first social networking website with name Dakhal.in, The website is not even one month old but it’s catching a fire like Facebook. Dakhal.in  is developed by a student of 8thStandard Shubam Wagh and His father Dilip Wagh who belongs from Nasik. with the collaboration of father Shubham created this website which could be a great competitor to other social networking sites especially in India. This pair proved again, age doesn’t matter if you have talent, you can do anything and achieve anything. In present era many website are joining Internet world every second, but the idea of making a website as competetor of Facebook is not everyone’s cup of tea.

Dakhal Looks similar to Facebook

Everybody knows that Facebook is most popular social networking site and people love to use it all the time, so dad-son pair decided to establish a website that give feeling of using Facebook with some more advanced and useful features. Owner of Dakhal Shubam is a 13 years old kid, studying in 8th class at Saint Francis School in Nasik learned these all stuff from his father. They claim that Dakhal is the first social networking website of India offering Entertainment, Chatting, Jobs & much more facilities.

Contents in Dakhal.in

Chatting is the soul of any social networking website and so it is must, They knew it very well so they provided easy chatting controls they also decided to add more information and features which are necessary for Indian citizens. Dakhal is launched about 1 month ago and started gaining popularity with remarkable speed.

Dakhal mainly having these  features and contents

 Chatting like Facebook  Entertainment  Ancestry (option to create Family Tree)  Jobs Procedure  My Village  Preparation School Examinations

 

How Developer Find contents

The idea of making a unique website is although very good but firstly they had to collect data, more and more ideas. Dilig Wagh (co-desgner of Dakhal) said his son Shubam has so much interest in Ancestry and his village. When he went to his village, he took some pictures of village  & there after he uploaded them on the website. Not only this for making it more good and uploading more material related to the ancestry, he went to cities of Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Gujrat for collecting the information about Ancestry. They met lots of people in these cities and find their way of living and their Ancestors.  

Academic & Job Help from Dakhal

On first first official social Network of India, detailed information regarding examination of Lok Seva with preparation of different examination along with Question papers for entrance examination are available. This will assists and play very important role for those who are looking to crack competition exams. Similarly in the field of Jobs like Banking, Defence, Engineering, the whole course & Selection procedure is also available in this website. There is a question paper in Dakhal.in which contains maximum of 50Marks. The candidate who secure maximum marks will be awarded with a sum of 2000 INR. Academic help for the students of class first to 10+2 with Engineering, management, Computer science, Aeronautics are also available with large number of additional courses . For making study more easy, the courses from Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) with a total of 20,000 courses are available in Video & Audio Formats. As Dakhal is so similar to Facebook in look andprovide really important and very useful features and content, it can be expected it will attain popularity like leading social networks soon.

Dakhal social networking limited service is an online service, platform, that focuses on facilitating the building of social networks or social relations among people in India and international also. on Dakhal you can share interests, activities, Videos/Images, or real-life connections, you can play online games learn using online education facility for all.Dakhal also allows its members to create their own family tree, share pictures and videos, organize family events, and search for ancestors.
