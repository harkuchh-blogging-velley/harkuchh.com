How to Get PR for My Blog ?

I’m sure you have heard of PR or Page Rank or Google Page Rank, these all are the same thing. As I said I’m sure you may have heard of Google Page rank, but.. there are only very few people who knows what exactly Google Page rank stands for. If you get high pr backlinks for your blog it take your blog to the great height. You don’t need to go for any buy back links from any paid link building service you can get PR in free just by following tips given here.



Having a blog or website in present Internet world is not enough, there are thousands of people over internet doing same thing you’re doing, so you must need to make your blog content visible enough to get maximum hits. Google PageRank is one of the most important factors that Google uses to determine the importance of any blog and it is strongly based on the value of your Blog’s inbound links. Google generally evaluates how many links there are to your blog from other pages, and the relevance and quality of the linking sites.

How to Get Google Pagerank

Getting pagerank or improve page rank is not everyone’s cup of tea. One need to utilize all techniques available in order to make their web presence attractive, unique and appealing so that it help them in `improve search engine ranking`. High Quality Content

Well, in blogging field it is most important to know that `Content is King` if you want Page Rank for your blog or you want increase Page Rank, then you must have high quality original content on your blog. If your blog will have original and valuable content then readers will love to spend their time reading your articles and sharing goes higher this way.

Guest posting

Guest posting is the most popular, trusted and effective way to get PR for a blog see when you writes a guest post for a blog having remarkable Page Rank, then you gets a high-quality, relevant inbound link to your website and Google gives it preference more than anything else to consider a blog for giving Pagerank.You must ensure that the blog where you’re going to publish your article is a reputed blog and it’s having good Page Rank.

Directory Submission

Directory submission is a bit time consuming process, but it is also true that in order to boost Page Rank you must have high quality Backlinks associated with your blog name and believe me submit your site on different web directories and article directories  is the best way to generate quality backlinks for any blog. Some popular Directories to submit your blog link are Yahoo Directory, DMOZ, Best Of The Web, Ezine Articles, Article Base.

Comment on Blogs

I think this is the easiest and most crucial step in getting and improving Google Page Rank. Blogging requires a Blogger very active socially. When you land on any blog for any kind of information make it your habit to leave a genuine comment there, because some Blogs are Do-Follow and they can help you to easily generate back links for your blog.

Social Bookmarking

I don’t think I need to explain importance of Social Bookmarking, as it is a most common effective way to get free traffic as well as increase Page Rank, look when we share our Site on different Social networks we gets decent traffic and also free Backlink. Most popular Social Bookmarking Sites are Facebook, g+, Twitter, Stumble Upon , digg.

Website Accessibility

This is the fact which most of the people don’t care about, but this plays very significant role in reputation of your blog in front of Google. It can be easily understood that, if a Website remains  down for a long period of time, Google slowly reduce the ranking of that particular site. So You must ensure that your Website is always up.

Regularly Updation

Imagine you’re a teacher and there are many student in your class, some comes for lecture occasionally where as some comes daily, then whom will you prefer as your best students, you know your answer very well, same like that Google really like blogs that keep their content fresh and unique. The more you post, Google crawls your site more and it simply increases chances for you to improve page rank.

Keywords and Tags

You may know that already, keywords and meta tags are one of the key factor for getting traffic to a site and it is one of the most crucial factors in the search engine ranking system. This step is quite taskful as searching of valuable keywords in never easy and once you get best keywords by following it’s searching and competition volume, use them strategically. This will boost your Traffic, ranking, earnings and yes Page Rank.

Inter Linking

Inter linking your posts is a way to provide access to a related posts from one post and it’s a very useful tool to get better ranking, it increases your websites accessibility so when you think you can link your any previous post to your new one must integrate them because this is a basic as well as effective technique for effective link building.

Engage Visitor

Nothing is more effective than this point, if you successfully keep your the visitors engaged in reading your Articles via interesting and likeable stuff. You can make your visitor stick to your blog by interactive site design, interesting feedback section and Videos which make the visitor watch them.
