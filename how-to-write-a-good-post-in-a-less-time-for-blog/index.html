How to Write a Good Post in a Less Time for Blog

Hey, This is a very important post for all bloggers, only bloggers knows What is a Post or Article and what is the worth of a good post, I know writing a good post for blog in few minutes is not possible for a new blogger or even for normal bloggers, a new blogger takes at least 2-3 hours to write a post normally. Well I must say Writing a blog post is not too difficult, anybody can write, You may have seen many who can write good on paper, but as a blogger writing a good post with unique contents is different. You need that quality which differentiate you and your blog from others and take you at the top.   You may know that ‘Post is the king of any website, and quality of posts on a blog is responsible for good rank, traffic, ratings, reviews and all.’ If your website or blog is full of unique and fresh contents, search engines will give value to your website. Now if you really want to be a good blogger and want to earn good money from Internet you must have to learn How to write a good post for your blog. Writing a good article is a daunting task undoubtedly, but by following the steps given below it will become much easier to you,  I have tried here to give my best answer for how to write a good blog post. Read them very carefully for best results.  

 Go Offline

The most first thing you have to do when you are going to write a post for your blog or website, Go offline from all social networking sites specially Facebook, google+ and Twitter.  While writing a post using Internet is must but along with it logging out the networking sites is also must. You may not believe it is fact that writing a single post with using facebook take that much time in which you can easily write three posts these are factors which disturb you while thinking and you will take a lot time to discover ideas and finding the best lines for post. Stop the songs and music also by doing so your concentration level will increase.  

Choose a Topic

In order to get started, you need to select a topic about which you have to write. In writing, there is always a broad variety of topics to write about, The easiest way to find a topic is just see your blog  category names and after that write anything related to your categories on Google, and when you hit the enter numbers of result come in front of you then select any good topic and just start working on it. If you write about everything on your blog then you can choose a good topic about latest fashion, gadgets, news, Blogging, Sports, Glamour, online marketing and so on. You can also decide the post in advance this will help your mind to engage with the topic and get more ideas.    

Take Time To Research, but don’t take too much

Well after choosing a topic for the post now it’s time to research, because it is impossible to explain each and everything perfectly about the topic you selected, so first search about it on net as more as you can, even when you think you know everything about a certain topic then also research because by doing this you will get more useful points and more ways to explain things. So take ample time to do your research before writing. But take care of it that you don’t waste too much time on it, make a balance.  

 Write complete Post first and then Edit

It’s up to you that you like to write in a friendly or professional tone, depending on the topic you have. When you start writing the post finally, Don’t write one line and then check it for corrections at that time you just write whatever coming in your mind, let the flow of thoughts come in. Just write a raw draft post include more and more important and valuable points in post with keywords. Once you finish the post take a rest for few minutes, go toilet and take water/tea/coffee etc it will give you a fresh mind same like recharging a mobile phone. Then sit again and start editing the post, remember It’s time to give a polished look to your post, while editing read the post very carefully check all the grammar and words placement.  

Don’t Force Yourself  to write more

In case you find that you’re stuck, don’t try to force the words to come, You just Save the post as draft and start working on something else. If any related inspiration strikes, then Edit that document again and add your ideas. You can even switch from one blog post to another, spending a few minutes on each as ideas comes to you. It’s a huge time-saver and trusted way to write good.  

Needs of a good blog post

After you finish writing and editing of the post now you have to focus on a very important factor text color and size, use different color and bigger size for highlighting main points in the post and always try to write in paragraphs don’t make the lines too congestive,

Using Bullet Points is also a great idea, This type of writing strategy will provide your readers ease and convenience as they read your article.

Keep It Simple Yet Sensible  In writing, it is a matter of informing your readers rather than trying to impress them with very profound words. Try to keep the words simple but with sense.

Be Original : The best things any blogger need to keep in mind write is to be original with all your contents. Using someone others contents on your own blog is considered as a crime.

 

SEO Your Post

Now all your task of writing, editing and polishing the post is completed and it’s ready to publish but don’t ever forget to SEO your new written post before publishing it, Search engine optimization is the best way to get a decent traffic from search engines like google, Yahoo, Msn etc. SEO includes writing catchy lines, title, adding needed images, alt text, meta data, etc. You can also a SEO plug-in to your wordpress or blogger account, it will make your work easy and need less efforts, read about how to improve traffic on you blog here.  

Publish the Post

All work have been done it’s time to rock with your awesome post, i.e. After completing all above things,now  it’s time to publish your draft blog post. If you are satisfied with all the stuff you wrote and feeling glad after writing a useful and valuable post, hit the publish button with the words, Ohh yeahhh :) and see you done :).  

Personal Advice

One more thing I wanted to add for those who are really serious about their work and blogging, If you want to see your blog on top and your blog posts on nowhere other than  first page of the google and all search engines, be dedicated to blogging properly, fix your priority and set your passion for it, You may already know that blogging is now really a big field people earning lakhs and more monthly from blogging, if you don’t then kindly click here. There is no substitute of hard work, In blogging results may take time to come but once it start it will never stop so don’t loose your confidence, and always be positive.   If you will Follow these steps properly I can say you are on your way to write  brilliant Post in a small part of the time for sure, I love to know that Guys, if these points or this post make your work even a lil bit easy and faster, hit on like and share if you like what you see, please comment about your experience. Thank You.
