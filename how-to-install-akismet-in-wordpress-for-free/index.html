How to Install Akismet in Wordpress for Free !!

Today in the evening I was talking with one of my friend, she told me to see a post on her blog I saw it, and it was good then I tend to write a comment but suprisingly there was no any option for comment on her blog, I asked her the reason for that and she said ”through comments so many spams come into my blog so I removed the comment option”, I said then use Akismet and stay away from spams, she said it’s very complex to install Akismet and it’s paid also, I said no it’s not then she asked me Really !! tell me how to do so, Here is the answer..  

What is AKISMET ?

Akismet is a free spam filtering software for blogs. Akismet filter link spams from blog comments. When you have Akismet installed in your WordPress and someone comment on your blog with spam links, akismet don’t allow it to enter in your database and it will keep your account safe from Comment based hacking. Akismet is offered by Automatic, the corporation which employs most of the main developers of the Free Software weblog platform WordPress.  

How to Install Akismet ?

Installing Akismet is very simple but getting Akismet API Key for it’s registration is lil bit complex and just because of that people ignore to install a very useful software in their web blog, here is the complete procedure to install Akismet and get API Key for registration.  
  1. Go to Plugins, Click on Add New.
  2. Write Akismet and click on search.
  3. Click on Install Now and then Activate.
  4. Click on enter your Akismet API key (on top of screen).
  5. Now click on Get an Akismet API key (in blue box).
  6. Sign Up with $0-$120/yr plan.
  7. Fill your Name and e-mail in the box.
  8. Don’t forget to drag Akismet worth button to $0.00 and click on Continue.
  9. It’s Done ! you have got an email from Akismet, open it.
  10. Copy the Akismet API key and paste it into wordpress Akismet plugin and click on Update options>>.
