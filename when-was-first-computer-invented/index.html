When Was First Computer Invented...

In ancient times man used sticks, bones and peddles for countingbut as the time passed many machines were developed to solve the purpose of calcilations. The first instrument developed for counting purpose was ABACUS. Several calculating devices were developed before the modern day computers, came into existence. Let us see at glance. Abacus was the first calculating device made in chinain 50BC. It could  add and substract only….

2. Napier Bones
