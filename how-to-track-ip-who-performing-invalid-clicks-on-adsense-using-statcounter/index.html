How to Track IP Who Performing Invalid Clicks on Adsense Using StatCounter

Just 2-3 days back I wrote a guest post for Ammar Ali’s  blog Allbloggingtips with title `Why and How To Protect Adsense Account From Invalid Clicks?` and because I wrote that post so Ammar sir gave me privilege to handle all the comments below that post, when I visited that page next day I saw many comments at the bottom, most of them thanked Author(me) for sharing that useful info but there I noticed almost every person saying `Hey Thanks K.K this is really helpful, I’m also having a serious issue of invalid clicks on one of my websites these days you have given awesome info. kindly also tell us how to detect those IP’s from where invalid clicks are being performed on our blog`, so this post is dedicated to all those bloggers who are keen to know `How to track invalid Adsense clicks using StatCounter`. Every Adsense publisher knows that Invalid Click Activity on Adsense Ads is really a very serious issue which can lead them to a banned Adsense account from Google and painful thing is that that ban remains permanent. Google may knows everything and it can give you result of any query but still it doesn’t provide any tool to detect the IP’s from where Invalid Clicks or Click Bombing have done. So it’s all publisher’s duty to fetch those IP’s to protecting their AdSense account from Invalid Click Activity. Many webmasters and bloggers have lost their AdSense account just because of Invalid Click Activity and their unawareness, but I’m sure you’re aware enough that’s why you are reading this article on Harkuchh.com, so let’s discus how to track the IP address from where you are getting Invalid Clicks on your Ads. You can track every single IP performing clicks on Adsense Ads by using StatCounter, and it’s pretty easy. All you need to do is read and follow the given steps very carefully, must note that You can’t afford to miss any point. Let’s start..

Track invalid Adsense Clicks using Statcounter

1. Create an account on Statcounter by using your permanent email account. 2. After that you’ll need to create a new project for your website which you want to track. Click on Add New Project and in Project url fill your website URL and fill all the required information like, Project Title, Country, Time Zone, etc. Just select Invisible Tracking and you can choose to receive monthly or weekly reports via email.   3. Once you’re done with the previous step, StatCounter will detect the platform of your website and gives you a code with detailed instructions on how to install the stat counter code in your website, just follow the given instruction carefully.

How to Track IP clicking on Ads

Now once you have set statcounter for your website by following the given instructions, you can start viewing all your site related reports within minutes. Now back to the Statcounter Home page. 4. First of all click on your project name (your site) then click on Stats and next then click on Exit Link Activity here you can find all the Links on which your visitors clicked to Exit from your site. You can find date, time, exit link name and also the page on which the exit link was clicked. Exit link means the link by clicking which a user jumps to a new page that might be your Ad or a link to any other website. If you will examine the links clicked you can find how many clicks are performed there on advertisements and also at what time the click was made. So there you get all the clicks performed on your Ads but this information is not enough because still you are far from those IP’s from where clicks are done so let’s move further, look at the opened page you can easily see a Magnifying glass icon before the date in every row of the table. By clicking on that magnifying glass you will get to the page where you can see more detailed information about the visitor who clicked on the link. You can find the IP address of the clicker(s) here along with his location, host name, browser, OS, resolution, ISP, visiting length, and the pages he viewed including the exit link. Now if you use a bit of your awesome brain you can track all the valid as well as invalid clicks on your Adsense Ads. If you find any IP clicked on your Ads many times it simply means you are being click bombed by that IP, then you can disable ads temporarily on your website or remove your website from authorized websites list in Google AdSense, you can also ban the fetched IP for showing your Ads from cPanel of your site. Hey publisher, it is highly recommended that whenever you feel any sudden or suspicious change in your Adsense Account  like sudden increase in Page CTR or many clicks but Zero or very low CPC then your most important task is to detect the location and IP address of the person doing invalid click activity on your Ads. This is highly recommended because in case you unfortunately get banned from AdSense because of invalid activity on Ads, you will have stats which you can use at the time of appeal for your account being reinstated. I hope if your question was how to check someone’s IP or how to find those IPs who are doing froud clicks, this complete tutorial to featch the IP of invalid clicker on Ads will help you to keep your Adsense safe and along with your forever, have a look of my other Adsense tutorials those will be also helpful for you.
