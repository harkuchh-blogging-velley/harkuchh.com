How to Create Fan Page on Facebook

Today a very  large number of people use Internet and most of them use social networking sites like Facebook, Orkut, Twitter, Omagle etc and we know maximum people are those who are using Facebook to connect with the near and dears live in any corner of the world, some people who use Facebook, make Facebook fan pages, some make it for their interest and some make pages on Facebook for promoting their business.  Now if doing blogging or any business then you must have a fan page on Facebook, in order to create your own page on FB just give 2-3 minutes to this posts, it provide you the simplest way to create a Facebook fan page..

Simplest Procedure of Creating page on Facebook

1. Log in to your Facebook Account. 2. Write pages into Facebook search bar. 3. Click on Facebook pages 4. Click on top right Option Create Page. 5. Choose your needed category. 6. Fill required info about your page. Click on Get Started…. You Done ! Now what are you waiting for, your page is created, start working on it and start promoting your Business, Blog, Website or any product, I think we are blessed this service is free, and it’s effective.
