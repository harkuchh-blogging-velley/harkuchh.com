Most Important Plugins for Wordpress Blog

Hey Bloggers ! If you are reading this post it simply means you are a Blogger, because I know people who don’t related of Blogging never open such post, Blogging became very popular in last half decade and most important fact is that making blog or website by using differ languages like HTML and PHP is past now, now most of the bloggers and designers use CMS (Content Management System), as it works too fast and easily. In many CMS’s wordpress is the most popular, even all the top bloggers use and suggest WordPress to every newbie blogger.   Today even I’m a new category Blogger but I understand one thing that, Plug-ins play a very important role for any blog, now some people may ask ‘What is plugin’ so let me tell you, plugins are the tools which enhance the performance as well as look of a blog, I’m going to share the list of most important plugins which is must required by any blog, These all are free and open-source plugins, here we go..  

Here is the list of WordPress Plugins which your Blog must need

  1. All in one SEO pack : SEO (Search engine optimization) is very important for any Blog and this is probably the best SEO plugin for WordPress blog. It is easy to use and responsible for your posts to come on the search engines first page like Google, Yahoo, Bing etc. There are many SEO plugins but we strongly recommend you to use this one.    2. Google Analytics : For a blogger getting traffic and visitors reports is very necessary, and there is no any plug-in better than Google Analytics for this, you just need to create your account in Google Analytics, and verify ownership of your website by adding a tracking code in WordPress. This show clean and explained report of every visit on your website like time of visit, duration, page views, total visits, countries, Traffic Source (Direct, Refferal or Social) and many more.    3. Akismet : Akismet is a free spam filtering software for blogs. Used by millions, Akismet is quite possibly the best way in the world to protect your blog from comment and trackback spams, it filter link spams from blog comments and don’t allow them to enter in your database and it will keep your account safe from Comment based hacking. Simply we can say it offer decent spam protection to your blog.   4. Ad Injection :This is the Simplest and probably the best plugin for placing ads on your website or blog, this plug-in have a very simple user interface so that anyone can understand how to use it, You just have to paste ad codes in this  and then you won’t need to paste ad codes in any post in future, it will place the ads automatically,It also Restrict who see the ads by post length, age, referrer or IP and it’s Cache compatible too.    5. Captcha : Do you know guys most comment you see in your WP dashboard are fake, some programmers and hackers make a comment script (robot) and leave it on internet to comment on various sites and it is the most common way to hack any website, so Captcha Plugin intended to prove that the visitor is a human being not a spam robot. This Plugin asks the visitor to answer a math question and only a human can do so.   6. Sexy Bookmarks : After Publishing a post on blog sharing it everywhere is very important especially sharing the recently published post’s links on Facebook, g+, Twitter, Digg and StumbleUpon is very very important need of any blog post. Sexy Bookmarks is a nice tool for share posts on all social networking websites, install this and enjoy sharing in one click. You can see this below this post.    7. nrelate Related Content : It’s Quite important thing, suppose a visitor come to our blog with the reference of Search engine, in this case he reads the post and close the site but if we show him the related content of the post he read at the end, he may like to know more and click on related post by this way we get more page views, visit time as well as good reputaion by search engines. So if you also want this use this plugin.    8. W3 Total Cache : This is possibly the best caching plug-in for WordPress. Many bloggers prefer to use Hyper Cache but W3 Total Cache does much more,  it let’s blogger minify the scripts, cache database queries, add expiration tags to static images and it will definitely help improve your site’s score as far as page speed is concerned and more.    9. Google XML Sitemaps : I must say if you are a Blogger then you must want that Google and all other popular search engines to know about every single post and page of your WordPress blog, and for this you need a XML sitemap as this plugin let you generate one in a click.    10. WP Optimize : Only a few knows about this plug-in, but it is a very important plugin for every blog, WP Optimize plugin cleanup WordPress database tables. It can post revisions, trash spam comments and optimize MySQL tables automatically, another best thing is it works more efficiently and consumes less space.    11. Login Logger : This plugin logs the IP address and the exact date & time when a user logs into your WordPress dashboard. It also keeps track of any unsuccessful login attempts. Use this plugin in combination with Limit Login Attempts to automatically lock out users after they have made ‘n’ unsuccessful login attempts.    12.WP-PageNavi : I personally recommend every Blogger to use this Plug-in, it’s a cool paging navigation tool J,Yeah really ! This plug-in shows page numbers at the bottom of the home page instead of showing <<Older posts or newer posts>>. It make the visitors work easy and give a better way to jump on any page with one click only.   13. Bing 404 : This plug-in will display links to relevant article on the error page when a visitor open link of your website and lands on a 404 page for which redirection is not enabled.    14. Pubsubhubbub :This is also a useful plug-in, When a blog post is published,this plug-in will instantly ping Google and that is a strong signal to the search engine for index your article. i.e. it helps you to beat content scrapers using Fat Pings.    15. WordPress PDA : Many people access internet through mobile phones, and if you want mobile users on your website too then use this plug-in, it help serve the mobile version of your blog based on their browser’s user agent, means the same site will be serve to both desktop and mobile users. You can also use Wptouch plug-in for same purpose. Wp Touch makes your website phone or smartphone friendly. Easily access able in iPhone and smartphone etc.  

I Strongly recommend these plug-ins because these are most popular in all top bloggers and used my millions. These plug-ins make our work task free and gives a great look to our blog, Only precaution you have to take care is don’t use fake (duplicate) plug-ins, you must check that plug-in you are installing is from official WordPress or a trust worthy site.
