Tips & Requirements for Get Approved Adsense Account

Many people owning many blogs and websites and most of them doing that thing for getting income, Google Adsense is the most famous name for earning through the Internet. Google Adsense provide Ads to the Blogs and sites and when a visitor visit to the blog He/She see Ads over there If they like to know more about the thing shown in the Ad and open it by clicking on it, then Google provide some Income to the Adsence Account from where the Ads were placed on the Blog or website.

The hardest part of getting started with Adsense is getting Google to approve your website. In fact  for many people even  it is easier to get a loan of $100,000 from bank than getting a approved Google Adsense account :) . The solution of such problem is to make your blog prepared before you apply for Adsense.

You can’t start using Google Adsense on your Blog until you are approved by Google. The very first thing you must do is read Carefully Adsense Program Policies & Terms of Service (TOS) and also very important Terms and Conditions. If your site doesn’t fall within the guidelines that Google sets out, it will be denied.

Some very important points are listed below which every webmaster or Blogger need to keep in their mind and follow them carefully before applying for Google Adsense Account..

Points need to focus for approved by Google Adsense :

  • Your Blog should not be Under Construction it shoud be running.
  •  Website Fulfill almost every points mentioned on Program Policies & Terms of Service (TOS).
  • Website or Blog Getting  decent Traffic.
  • There is no too many pages on your Blog.
  • There is no Copyrighted data on your blog from any other blog use original content, write for your blog by yourself.
 Now you may understand Creating a website is not a big deal at all, the real challenge is to get traffic and that too from search engines so if your blog has very less traffic than wait few months and than apply for Adsense. Your website should be according to the Terms and Conditions of Google make it sure there is  no adult content, warez, gambling sites, Hacking and hate spreading contents & Copyrighted Data or post on your website. Be Regular on updating Post try to upload a new post in 3-4 days on your blog  if it’s not possible for you than fix a time interval for uploading posts and follow them sincerely, regularly updating Blogs attracts Google and If they find useful contents on your blog your account will be approved without any problem.

 Quality and Original  Content

Quality Content is the Best than anything on a blog,  as a famous writer said “It’s Good to publish a single quality Article once in a week than Publishing Scrap Daily”. This is very simple to understand If Post or Content on your Blog is Original (not copyrighted) and written in the best manner then your Application will be accepted very easily by the Adsense Team. You can take ideas from any blog or website but you can’t Copy and paste them on your blog otherwise You will never Get  an Approved Adsense Account.  

Simple and Easy Design

 Before submitting your site for Adsense make sure that the design you use in the site is a simple one with no much graphics works also it is nice if you have a easy navigation process in your site and try to avoid other ad networks while the reviewing process is going on, its not a compulsory one but a simple factor which may  help you to get the Adsense.

Also remember to avoid any kinds of adult, hate spreading and Hacking contents since Adsense won’t approve any sites that is having such contents.  

 Apply Once, wait for Reply

Now If you think that your Blog is ready for submitting to Adsense after reading the points given above then you go to Adsense  Sign up Page and then submit complete information about your Blog & yourself and make it sure you  fill only real personal information.

After sending application to Adsense you will get a reply e-mail from Adsense Team regarding they partially checked your website after that  follow the procedure given in the mail.  If You can’t received any mail from them then Don’t worry you will be surely respond, sometimes it takes time because they have millions of application from the bloggers all over the world. Generally they reply in maximum 4 days, sometime they reply in 2 days or even 1 day.

If your application got Disapproved by the Adsense Team, don’t start Crying :D Don’t worry they mention point why your request got  disapproved just fulfill the requirement (mentioned in point)  and apply again because they have lot of applications and they give preference to only Best. Some people when get disapproval mail they send another request instantly  from different email account without taking care of the point in the mail, you don’t do this foolish thing because your blog was not checked by any person their machine or Software check blogs and Website and don’t matter how many email accounts you use for submitting application they only approve your blog if their machine and Software declare it okay.

By fulfilling all these points and following steps you will get an approved Google Adsense Account easily in maximum 4-5 days. This post give the complete information of many Adsense related questions like, What are requirements for a blog approved by Google Adsense, Tips to get approval for Google Adsense & Best way to get approved by Google Adsense..

Note :

Use your real name instead of using any nick or fake name in application if You need to be 18 years old or above, and always remember Even after your blog got approved and you start running Google ads on your blog or website you can’t post anything on it, if you break the TOS, your Adsense account will be removed or possibly banned. Read and obey very carefully Adsense Terms and Conditions, it may take your time but it is very necessary for good business with Google. as we said in the earlier post  What is Blogging, Why Blogging becoming Popular in Youth ? never think money is the fruits for your hard deeds but to earn these fruits, you need to show dedicated efforts. Always remember good things takes time.
