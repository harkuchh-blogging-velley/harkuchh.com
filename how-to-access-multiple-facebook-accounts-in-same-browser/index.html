How to Access Multiple Facebook Accounts in a Browser

Is Accessing multiple Facebook account through same browser simultaneously possible ? May be you are thinking this, after reading the title of this post. so let me tell Oh Yeah… this is possible and it’s very easy too.   Many time when we are using Facebook account we have to open another account so for this we generally use any other browser, but now you won’t need this, here is the solution of this problem, Go through this post and you will get it very easily.

One and only requirement for this scheme is that you should use Google Chrome browser, so install the Google Chrome if you are using any other and follow these steps..

  1. Open your facebook account by the same way you log into your account normally i.e. by filling your User ID and Password on facebook homepage now you are logged in.   2. Now we need to open another account in same browser then Right Click on Facebook icon at top left corner and just click on Open link in Incognito window. like shown in picture below  

  3. After clicking on Open link in Incognito window a new Facebook login page will be open in same browser fill your another User ID and Password and enjoy the experiment ;)  

What is Incognito Window 

In Incognito window any data, website  or Pages you view won’t appear in your browser history or search history, and they won’t leave other traces, like cookies, on your computer after you close all open incognito windows. but any files you download or bookmarks  will be preserved.   May be this scheme is also working with other browsers too, but I haven’t tried this so I suggest you everyone to try it with all the browsers you are using, and also share with us if it is possible with any other.
