How to Manage Blog and Blogging During Exams

Exam time is close now and this is the most popular question for bloggers category that ‘How to deal with blog and study during exams consistently’ and it’s like a task for all bloggers to manage blog and study during exams, as a blogger you can’t stop blogging for long time interval and you also can’t ignore academic exams because these are very important. So the question is `How to manage Blog and Exams Together` if you ask this to people mostly will suggest you to stop or ignore blogging until your exams get over but I recommend you to don’t do that otherwise it will effect your site ranking, traffic and earnings as well. I have great solutions for this problem which I follow during my engineering exams, and trust me, they work always like a charm for me to effectively manage my blog during exams and I’m 100% sure they will also help you a lot, here are the..  

5 Simple Ways To Manage Your Blog During Exams

 

 1.   Write Posts in Advance and Schedule them for later

WordPress, Jhoomla and all CMS give us ability to schedule our post to publish later automatically then why don’t we take advantage of this technology in need, You just need to write some posts in advance and schedule it to publish during exams this is the best way to manage your blog during exams.    

 2.   In holidays between exams give little time to your Blog

No matter how busy you are during exams, spend sometime to write quick blog posts. Just think it’s not a big deal, as we all know, no one do study 24*7 during exams specially bloggers can’t, just make a strong promise to yourself that you will not waste your time on social sites and will sit on PC for just a little time and in that little time you must have to complete and publish a post. This idea really works. You just need to be honest to your self promise.  

 3.   Publish your already published old but useful post as new

This is one of my favourite idea it’s lil bit creative but very easy and smart, all you need to do is that during exams you just need to give maximum 5-10 minutes to your blog and publish your an old post again as new about which your visitors don’t know or rarely know, if you do that with minor editing if required, your visitors will think you working regularly on it, this way your regular readers will be regular on your blog.  

4.   Seek Your Fellow Bloggers for Help

When a blogger do blogging regularly then he automatically come in contact with many bloggers and building a good relationship with fellow bloggers is very important. You can ask your friend blogger to manage your blog during exams, like writing articles, accepting guest posts, approving comments, this can be done by paying some money to him or doing it for free depend on your friendship.  

 5.   Ask your real life close Friends to help

You can also ask to your real life close friends for writing posts about various topics, discus with  them the topics on which you need posts and if it’s OK with your friend then it’s great otherwise you can also ask them to write on a topic they can write easily, by this way you will have many posts for your blog and you can publish them on your blog in exams by moderating it in your taste. I’m thnkful to my dear friend Raj who helped me in same category. I’m sure you also have some nice friends like me.     Don’t miss : Simple way to create a Subdomain of your blog or website By following these ways you never have to worry too much about your blog in even exam days or whenever it’s not easy for you to work on blog. This way will not only give you free time when you’re busy they also make it easy for you to manage your blog when you don’t have enough time. :lol: Hope you enjoyed reading this article, now if you have any other ideas to manage blog and exams please let me and the readers know about them.
