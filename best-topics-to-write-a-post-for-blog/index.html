Best Topics to Write Posts for Blog

We have talked a lot about blogging in our blogging category, in posts What is Blogging, Top Blogs & Bloggers, Adsense Tips, how to write post in less time etc but I observed from my personal experience and readers comments and also from chats with bloggers on facebook and Twitter, that making a blog is not a task, it’s easy, anyone can make a blog and run it on Internet.   The problem is to picking up the blog to top means getting good ranking, decent traffic and remarkable income, Many of my friends and collegue bloggers always complaining that their blogs are fail in getting traffic from search engines, Well for getting good traffic you must need to write something which attaract the internet users especially the youth, and if your problem is which niche to choose for best post, stop worrying here we are going to share all topics which are most in demand and you can say evergreen topics for writing posts, have a look..  

10. Health

Today is the time of technology so the world going online, people tends to find solutions for their health related matters online. If you have a enough knowledge on medicine and various fitness related issues and you think that you have potential to provide people great information and solutions for their health problems and giving them worth reading and valuable tips regarding fitness and Health, then you will see a great traffic difference on your blog in just a short time. For example You can share post like How to make your body strong and fit with staying on home.    

9. Fashion

Well We know Health and fitness topics are mostly searched only by those who are either suffering from some disease or they want to learn tips for their fitness, but above health, comes Fashion. You will be agree with me if I say “Knowing about health is a need but learning all about fashion is an addiction”. If you have intereset in glamour world and you love to read and write about Fashion trends which leading the society, believe me you can really make huge money with it. People from all around the globe use internet as the best and most easiest way to know about latest fashion and also follow it.    

8. Relationships

No surprise in this - most of the people have emotional part in their personalities which leaves dark effect on their entire life. People takes internet as their best friend the most secure way to improve their relationships, they do believing in the idea of self-aiding learning online about professional working relationships, love relationships and also having advises from people around the world. So it will be a boon for your blog if you start writing good content on relationship.    

7. Finance

Money, most probably the only thing which will remain Man’s love till the end of this existing world. Money is the essential need of everyone and this need make common nature of people - to learn ways to double their money encourage them to search about such methods, so Blogs with domain related to ways of improving finances and helping them setting up their own business, telling ways to manage their money always attract them, and it help in generating good traffic for a blog and also good revenue.    

6. Question and Answer Blogs

It’s a simple theory remember your achool days to make this concept clear, just think which teacher you preferred to visit the most in times of need, the one who always had a solution of your problems or the one who always use the words come later ? I know what your answer is and I hope We all have same views about it. Similarly, starting a blog or writing posts with niche of active question and answer session will easily generate remarkable revenue. People love to come that places where they can be listened and can get their answer for their queries. For best example we can see answer.com, so you can start a question and answer blog and make great income through it.    

5. Celebrities

Celebrities is always considered as the easiest and the most interesting topics to adopt in a blogging career to start writing reviews, updates, latest photos, their videos (specially uncenssored :P) and everything associated with them. The best part for this category is for writing about them you won’t have to be highly qualified, all you need to do this, is your interest in entertainment world.    

4. Buy and sell

You may have familiar with olx.com or many online shopping websites, now a days Online shopping has taken up the physical market with a great pace, you just need to choose what you need or what want direct from your computer table (or also bed if you using laptop :P) and get it delivered at your doorsteps. If you start providing such facility of online shopping with best deals you can easily get bulk of visitors each day.    

3. Social Media

Social media is a became a need now, do you know any person who use internet regularly and won’t have any account on any social site, you know your answer very well. Today people without using social sites feel always something wrong and even incompleteness in the day, one might not have a bank account but must be having three- four accounts on different social networking websites especially Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn. So if you read interesting, creative and valuable content related to social media people will love to visit your blog again and again because People want to know each and everything about their social media. 2. Technology

As I already said this is the technology generation, a latest technology become older the next day this is the field I think changing with the most speed, just remember time of five years back only few reputed people having mobile phones but today everyone have a latest technology mobile phone in pocket (even I have a cellphone :P) People In this Hi-tech world, staying up to date with the latest technology been revealed in the market is mandatory and they do regulalry suffer on internet for searching these things, the biggest benefit of choosing this niche as your blogging topic is, whenever a new gadget or device launch in to the market, it automatically creates a hype, big enough to bring visitors to your blog to know more about it. This will give you huge traffic as well as huge income.
