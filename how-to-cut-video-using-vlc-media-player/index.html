How to Cut Video Using VLC Media Player

Hey folks ! It happen many times at the time of watching a video or movie we like some part of it for example a songs, some great seens from the movie, comedy acts etc and we want to separate out that part from videos easily, in simple words it is called as video cutting.  Generally people use various softwares for video cuting, but I’m here to tell you that, to cut a small segment of a video you don’t have to install any software program on your computer system. For doing this, all you need is just VLC Media Player which I think everybody have on their system.  

How to Cut Videos Using VLC Media Player

Step : 1. Play the video in VLC player and click on `View`then select `Advanced Controls`. Step : 2. Now you can see some extra icons just above the Play/Pause button at bottom left corner. The first button from left is the Record button, just go to that point from where you want to cut a video then click on `Record` Press it again to stop recording.   After you finished cutting the video clip, you can find those clip in your “Documents” or `Video` folder named as VLC Record with the date and time. This is the best and simplest way to cut video, so from now whenever you find any seen great in a movie use the tool.
