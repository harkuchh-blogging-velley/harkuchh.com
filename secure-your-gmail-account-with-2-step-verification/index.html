Secure Your Gmail Account With 2-Step Verification

This is something very important for everyone I think. Securing your gmail (e-mail) accounts is one of the most important task while using internet. Now you can secure your Gmail account by using 2-step verification it is preety simple and really very effective tool to keep your emails secure from anybody other than you. In this login procedure would need more than just your password to open your account and access any mail from there it’s like a strong protective layer to gmail account.    The 2-Step verification procedure for Gmail account is very easy, you just need to follow some steps to enable that strong security feature to your email account. You can either choose to receive the 6-digit security codes on your mobile phone via text messages or a Voice call from Google.   To get started you just go to your Gmail account’s security page and Enable 2-step verification. Here is the complete procedure, follow this :  
    1. Sign in to your Gmail account. 
    2. Click on Account and then Security.
    3. There you will see 2-step verification, Click on Edit.
    4. Now Click on See how it works then Start Setup.
    5. Enter your Phone number and the mode in which you want to recieve code (Text or Call).
    6. Click on Send Code, (you will receive a Code on your mobile in mode you selected)
    7. Enter the Code and click on Varify.
    8. Click  on Confirm on the next page you will see (confirmation page).
