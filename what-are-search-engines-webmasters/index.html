What Are Search Engines Webmasters ?

Every top, popular and reputed Search engines like Google, Bing, Baidu have their search engine webmasters, these are the tools through which a webmaster or any website owner directly register their websites to these top search engines. Registration of any website to the engine webmasters search are essential and are very helpful in finding many things relating to their websites and ownership verification, site crawling, site errors, such as 404 (Not Found), 500 (Internal server error), denied, keywords through which the traffic is coming from search engines that and many other things. Steps To Register a site on Google Webmasters

Registration To Google Webmasters

  • Sign in to your Gmail account.
  • Now Go to google.com/webmasters.
  • Click On Add Site Orange Button.
  • Choose Your Suitable Method To Verify Ownership of that website to know google that you owns that website.
    1. HTML Verification - Just download the html file from the page in front of you and upload the given HTML file to your website’s root folder via File Manager in cPanel or through FTP Client like filezilla in the ‘public_html’ folder.
    2. HTML Tag Verification – Click on it and you found a single line of code. Copy it and paste within the header section of your website.
    3. Google Analytics Script - Sign In Google Analytics and link it to your website.
  • Now Click Verify. Thus Ownership Verified
  • Now Go To Webmasters Tool Home and click on Add/Test Sitemap Button and put your website’s sitemap URL like http://Harkuchh.com/sitemap.xml.
That’s all, you Done and have fun… don’t forget to share.
