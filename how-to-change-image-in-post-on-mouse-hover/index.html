How to Change Image in Post on Mouse Hover

Hey Guys, we are bloggers and the most important thing we need is undoubtedly visitors to our blog for all good rank, traffic and Income off course! a visitor come to our blog and they like something there very much and for that they save a bookmark of the page to reach there in the future easily, so we need to make our post good look, attractive and cool and for that we have to add the features that people love to experience while being on our blog.  Few days back I visited to a blog and there I saw a very attractive thing and I realized I need to share it because this is easy and very useful for all the bloggers, this feature will give a gorgeous and unique look to your post, what is that ?  This is a great trick to make your blog post attractive, by using it the post can change image automatically of the post on hovering mouse over it .

How To Change Image On Mouse Hover

Doing this is very easy, to use this awesome hover image change effect you need to use a simple html code. That html code is given Below and you just need to paste the given code wherever in your post you want to use this effect.  

Here is the HTML code

<img onmouseover=”this.src=’http://harkuchh.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/Best-tips-to-make-your-life-Beautiful-Malala-Yousufzai.jpg’” onmouseout=”this.src=’http://harkuchh.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/How-to-make-my-life-Happier.jpg’” alt=”” src=”http://harkuchh.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/How-to-make-my-life-Happier.jpg” />   Now you need to use your head a little bit, first of all upload the images in library which you want to use in the post, open those images in other tab and and copy their URL, and then paste the url of default image you want in post at the place of green link (just select green link part and paste url of your image - 2 times) and url of the image which you want on mouse hover will be pasted at the place of red link. Come on, it’s just look a bit complex but it’s damn easy just try it, it will make your post very effective and catchy. Don’t forget to share.
