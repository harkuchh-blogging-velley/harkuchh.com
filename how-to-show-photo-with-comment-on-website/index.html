How to Show Photo With Comment on Website

“When I comment on any website or blog then a logo come in front of my name, but I have seen with comments of many people there photo come with there name at the place of logo, I also want that when I comment on any website then my Pic come there with my name instead of that ugly logo, How to do that ?”



This was a message I received from a guy few days back, I explained to him all procedure then another guy asked me same question last evening, then I realised writing a post about this will be better instead of explaining same thing again and again to different people. So here is the Procedure to show your photo with your comment at the place of logo..

this is very easy and simple and not require more than 3-4 minutes.

  • First of all you go to Gravtar.com and fill your email address there.




  • Then go to your e-mail box there you will find a mail from Gravtar just click on it and your account is created. Now just fill your username and password.

  • Upload your Photo which you want to show with your comment, then click Next and you will see `Choose a rating for your Gravatar` option with 4 options, click on rated G (G rated Gravatar is suitable for display on all websites with any audience type). Procedure is 90% completed.
  • Now you go to your WordPress Dashboard and click on users there fill your First name, Last name name and Username and your email address ( fill same email, with which you created your Gravtar account). Eventually click on Update profile.
You done ! From now whenever you will comment on a website there your photo will shown with your name and comment. Now say, I love Harkuchh.Com :)
