What is Direct Ads marketing

Well it’s preety easy to understand, all you need is bit common sense ! Do you know, in the procedure of Adsense Ads google plays a broker role, suppose Apple is going to lauch a new featured iPad in market then Apple contacts Google for promote that New iPad and Apple pay 3$ to google for one promotion of their product. Then Google shows that iPad on our blogs and website via Adsense Ads and when a visitor of our blog click on that iPad Ad it’s counted as one promotion ( 1 click) and google gives us 2$ for that click, but Apple paid 3$ to google, then where do the remained 1$ gone, well it gone to the Google’s account for promoting the product via it’s Adsense Ads Service. Same thing happens in direct marketing but without the presence of Google i.e. companies contacts the blog owners directly without broker and they pay a fix amountof money for placing their product on blog in the form of image and link ads, and there is no Google to take credit of that amount.
